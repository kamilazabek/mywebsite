Animals collection

Here are the animals I had the opportunity to see in the wild. Observing animals in their natural habitat is one of my favorite travel activities. Hover on a photo to view interesting facts about a given species.

Humboldt penguins, native to the coasts of Peru and Chile, are one of the few penguin species that nest in burrows or guano caves to escape the heat of their warm, desert-like habitat.

Penguin in Peru

Humpback whales create complex, haunting songs that can last up to 30 minutes and travel for miles through the ocean, with all males in a region singing the same evolving tune.

Whale in Peru

Orangutans are the only great apes native to Asia and are so intelligent they have been observed using tools and even crafting umbrellas from leaves to stay dry.
